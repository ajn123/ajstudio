Hello, My name is Alex or my parents call me A.J because I love Apple Juice.
I am photographer in Falls Church Virginia who does portrait, event, and product **photography**.

I also do **videography**. Making videos has been a passion of mine since 2013 when I made my very first Youtube channel
teaching computer programming to the youth.

My engineering and photography background merge together with my love of the post production and editing process. I love spending hours in photoshop
and Premiere to make the most up to date products. I want to make every pixel count and retouch the photos until the customer is gleeful.

I believe in building a community and giving back in the DC/VA/MD area I want everyone to have a great experience with photos or film. If you feel
you do not have the budget at this time, feel free to reach out and I am happy to work something out with anyone.
